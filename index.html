Life and works

Samantha Hudson (born as Iván González in September 1999) is a Spanish artist, singer, actress, internet celebrity and LGBT activist. Her work has always been over gender and her relationship with faith and the Catholic church, due to her religious origins.

At 15, Samantha composed her first viral hit and videoclip Soy Maricón, as homework for school. In Soy Maricón she argues against the Catholic church and its position regarding sexual diversity in a very explicit language. That got her into some big controversies, being accused of blasphemy by a member of the Balearic Islands Parliament and also the video got to the bishop of Mallorca and got her excommunicated.

When she finished highschool, she moved to Barcelona for a short period of time. While there, in 2018 she started a change.org petition in order to claim the remains of the Spanish dictator Francisco Franco.

Since 2019, she lives in Madrid. From the beginning of 2019 she hold a drag performance in Sala Maravillas, sometimes joined py Paco Clavel, a famous artist from La Movida. Also that same year she released her album Los Grandes Éxitos de Samantha Hudson.

In 2021 she released her hew single Dulce y Bautizada mixed by the queer Spanish artist Putochinomaricón. She also has a new performance on Teatro Lara called "Eutanasia Deluxe", in which she performs her greatest hits. Also she will participate in the next edition of the Spanish version of Master Chef Celebrity.

To learn more about her, this is her Wikipedia page (only available in Spanish).
